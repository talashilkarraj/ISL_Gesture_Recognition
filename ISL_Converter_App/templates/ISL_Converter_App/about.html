MOTIVE OF THE PROJECT


GROUP NO: 5


Presented By:
MENTOR: Dr. Abhay Kshirsagar
GROUP MEMBERS:
Raj Talashilkar Grusha Shetty  Harshal Jouras  Aditya Rode

One of society's fundamental flaws is the social divide that exists between people who are disabled or handicapped and people who have all of their human faculties in working conditions. Communication, which is the foundation of human progress, is frequently an impediment for those who are unable to communicate their thoughts. According to a World Health Organization report, 63 million individuals in India suffer from complete or partial deafness, with at least 50 lakhs of them being children. While applications catering to American Sign language detection and conversion are readily available, people using Indian Sign Language are still in need of a mode of easy communication. This WebApp was hence developed with the goal of bridging the communication gap between the disabled and the rest of the population. With the help of this webapp deaf and mute people can communicate easily as it efficiently converts sign language into corresponding text and vice versa.
